🎧 Music Player Help

⬇ Offline Downloads

Tap the download icon next to a song or album to save it for offline listening. Downloaded music is available even without internet.

🎶 High-Quality Audio

Go to Settings > Audio Quality to choose between Standard, High, or Lossless playback options.

📱 App Layout

The bottom nav lets you switch between Home, Library, Search, and Settings. Swipe left/right to explore different sections.

✨ Smooth Animations

Enjoy subtle animations throughout the app to make your experience more fluid and enjoyable.

🆓 Free Users

Free users can stream music with ads and are limited to standard quality and online playback. Upgrade for more features.

▶ Controls

Use the playback bar to Play, Pause, Skip, or Rewind. Long-press on the track for more options like Add to Playlist.
